Lasagna

Description

Lasagne is a classic Italian al forno dish that generally consists of alternating layers of pasta sheets and various fillings, including - though not exclusively - ragù, besciamella and cheese, such as mozzarella, ricotta or Parmesan

Ingredients

  • 2 olive oil
  • 750g lean beef mince
  • 90g pack prosciutto
  • 800g passata or half our basic tomato sauce
  • 200 ml hot beef stock
  • nutmeg
  • 300g fresh lasagna sheets
  • white sauce
  • 125g ball mozzarella

Steps

  1. Brown the meat: Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large pan over high heat. Add 26.8 ounces lean beef mince and cook until browned, breaking it up as you go.
  2. Add prosciutto: Tear 3.2 ounces prosciutto into pieces and stir into the mince.
  3. Make the sauce: Pour in 3.3 cups passata and 0.8 cups hot beef stock. Add 1 pinch nutmeg. Stir well and simmer for 20 minutes
  4. Preheat oven: Preheat your oven to 190°C.
  5. Layer up: In a baking dish, layer meat sauce, 10.7 ounces fresh lasagna sheets, then 1 cups white sauce. Repeat until everything is used up, finishing with white sauce on top
  6. Add mozzarella: Tear 4.5 ounces mozzarella ball and scatter over the top.
  7. Bake for 35 minutes until golden and bubbling
